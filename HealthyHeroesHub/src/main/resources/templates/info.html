FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is HealthyHeroesHub?

HealthyHeroesHub is a community-driven app where users can share and discover nutritious meals. It features a calorie calculator to help you track your daily intake and a leaderboard showcasing the most popular meals based on user votes. Whether you're looking for meal inspiration or trending healthy recipes, this app helps you and your friends make better food choices.

How do I create an account?

To get started, click on Register, fill in your details, and create an account. Once registered, simply log in and explore the app—just make sure to follow the community guidelines.

How does the calorie calculator work?

The calorie calculator helps you assess whether you're eating the right amount of food daily. Just enter your height, weight, age, activity level, gender, and calories consumed throughout the day. The app will then calculate and inform you if you've eaten too much or too little.

How do I publish a meal?

To publish a meal, you first need to create a meal catalog—a themed collection of dishes. For example, you could create a "Vegan Dinner Specials" catalog and describe it, like: "A collection of my favorite vegan meals, inspired by my travels in France last summer." Once your catalog is set up, you can add detailed descriptions and share your meals with the community.

How does the leaderboard work?

The leaderboard ranks meals based on user upvotes, displaying the most popular dishes in descending order. This way, users can easily see the trending and highest-rated healthy meals.

Is there a report system in the app?

Yes! If you come across inappropriate content or behavior, you can use the built-in report system to flag it. This helps maintain a positive and supportive community focused on health and wellness.

How is the app moderated?

HealthyHeroesHub has an active moderation system where admins review content and user activity. If someone violates the app's guidelines, admins can restrict access.

Community Rules

Rules for Using HealthyHeroesHub

Rule Description
Honest Nutrition Information Always provide accurate details about the protein, carb, and fat content of meals. Misleading or false nutritional data can impact others' health and will not be tolerated.
Authenticity in Meal Catalogs Keep meal names and descriptions clear and genuine. Avoid excessive length, copying entire entries, using fake names, or spamming irrelevant details.
No Profanity Keep the language clean and respectful. Offensive or inappropriate language will not be tolerated.
No Harassment Treat all members with respect. Bullying, hate speech, or any form of harassment will result in immediate action.
Appropriate Content Only Do not post offensive, explicit, or inappropriate images or content. Keep it healthy and inspiring!
Be Supportive Encourage and motivate others. Negative comments or trolling will not be allowed.
No Spam Do not post irrelevant or promotional content. Keep the community focused on health and wellness.
Respect Privacy Do not share personal information about yourself or others.
Follow the Guidelines Violation of these rules may result in consequences, including permanent removal from the app.
